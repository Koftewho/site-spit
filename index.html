About

He was an aspiring rapper who posted music on Soundcloud.

DJ Spit is an aspiring singer/songwriter, but is often frustrated because nobody wants to listen to his music. He is depicted as somewhat absentminded, seen rhythmically mumbling "I'll knock your head off" to himself while standing idly on the sidewalk and consistently stumbling over his words. He is also extremely paranoid, accusing Pim and Charlie of messing with his head and promptly pulling a gun on them while losing his mind. He is often aggravated and/or aggressive, bordering on downright psychotic, and also routinely engages in criminal activies.
